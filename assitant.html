OpenAI Assistant, also known as GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer), is an advanced language model developed by OpenAI. It is designed to understand and generate human-like text based on the input it receives. OpenAI Assistant uses a variant of the Transformer architecture and has been trained on a massive dataset of diverse text from the internet, allowing it to generate responses that are contextually relevant and coherent.
