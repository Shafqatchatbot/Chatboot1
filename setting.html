1

Setting

The "setting" of OpenAI typically refers to the context in which OpenAI operates, including its goals, principles, and approach to artificial intelligence research and development.

2

Mission

Mission: OpenAI's mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence (AGI) benefits all of humanity. AGI refers to AI systems that possess the ability to understand, learn, and apply knowledge across a wide range of tasks and domains, akin to human intelligence..

3

Access and Accessibility

Access and Accessibility: OpenAI seeks to make its research, technologies, and tools accessible to a broad audience, including researchers, developers, policymakers, and the general public.

4

Collaboration

Collaboration: OpenAI collaborates with other research institutions, industry partners, and policymakers to advance the field of artificial intelligence and address societal challenges.
